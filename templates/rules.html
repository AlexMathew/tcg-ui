How to play

Remember the cricket trump card games we used to play years ago ? Relive the moments !

  • Hit "Start a new game"
  • Select the number of cards you want to play in each set
  • Hit "Heads" or "Tails" to call the coin toss
  • Hit the "Let's Begin" button to get started with the game
  • In each page, the player should make his move is displayed
  • Each player has three dropdown menus - Tests, ODIs and T20Is. Click the dropdown button to show the list of all stats for that format. Click one on of them to select and play that stat
  • The stats of the two players are compared and the corresponding changes are made
  • The game continues till one person's set is empty. To put it simple, the person who manages to collect all the cards wins.
